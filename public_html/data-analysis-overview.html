Data Analysis Overview

  • Data is being collected everywhere. Every transaction, every web page visited, every payment
  • Databases, relational and otherwise are being filled with raw data
  • Databases are now powerful engines for transforming data into useful information about customers and products and business practices
  • SQL and relational databases are a powerful combination that is useful in any arsenal of tools for analysis, particularly ad hoc basis
  • Data analysis is a process of inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and modelling data with the goal of discovering useful information, informing conclusions, and supporting decision-making
  • Data analysis has multiple facets and approaches, encompassing diverse techniques under a variety of names, while being used in different business, science, and social science domains.

Data Science

  • Data science is the practice of transforming raw data into actionable insight.
  • Trends of data science are
    • Data analytics
    • The Internet of things
    • Big data
    • Machine Learning
